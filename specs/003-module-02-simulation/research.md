# Module 2: Digital Twin (Gazebo & Unity) Research

## Simulation Fundamentals

- **Definition of Digital Twin**: A virtual representation that serves as the real-time digital counterpart of a physical object or process.
- **Importance in Robotics**: Enables safe testing, rapid prototyping, and cost-effective development without physical hardware.
- **Key Components**: Physical model, virtual model, data connection, analytics and insights.
